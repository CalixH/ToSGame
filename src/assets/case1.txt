Hidden Terms Price (PC Pitstop, 2005)

Source Agreement: cannot be found

What Happened? PC Pitstop, a software company, inserted a clause in their ToS stating that the first person to email them would receive $1,000. It took four months before someone finally claimed it.

Outcome: winner won $1,000

Reasoning: the contract explicitly stated the prize offer, making it legally enforceable

Evidence and Reasoning:

The ToS clearly contained a clause stating the reward

The company acknowledged that nobody read their ToS, proving their point
