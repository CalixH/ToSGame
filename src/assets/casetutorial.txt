Welcome to the Terms of Service Game!

Your mission is to assist clients in navigating deceptive Terms of Service agreements and End User Licensing Agreements. You'll receive emails outlining their cases and objectives, much like this one.

On the right panel, you'll find excerpts from the relevant Terms of Service or other contextual information. Your are required to select all pertinent clips and make a decision of how you want to proceed with each case.

Since each case is based off real events, you'll receive feedback on your choices and learn what actually happened after submitting your decision.

To begin, review the statements on the right and select those that seem awfully silly.
